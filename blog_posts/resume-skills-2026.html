Top Resume Skills to Add in 2026 (Data / MIS / BI Roles)

These are the most in-demand skills recruiters want to see on resumes in 2026—especially for Data Analyst, MIS, Reporting, and BI roles.

Your resume’s **Skills section** plays a major role in ATS shortlisting and recruiter decision-making. Adding the right skills—based on real job descriptions—helps you stand out immediately.

---

1. Technical Skills (Most Important)

  • Excel (Advanced) – Pivot Table, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, Power Query, Conditional Formatting
  • SQL – Joins, subqueries, window functions, CTEs
  • Power BI – DAX, data modelling, Power Query, report publishing
  • Python (Basics) – Pandas, NumPy, data cleaning
  • Tableau – dashboards, viz best practices
  • ETL Tools – SSIS, Airbyte, ADF (optional)
  • Databases – MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQL Server
⭐ Tip: Prioritize skills based on the job description (JD) keywords.
---

2. Analytical Skills (2026 Demand)

  • Data Cleaning & Transformation
  • Data Visualization
  • Trend & Pattern Analysis
  • KPI Development
  • Business Problem Solving
  • Root Cause Analysis
  • Forecasting & Basic Statistics
✔ Add measurable impact in your experience to highlight these skills.
---

3. Domain Skills (Very Important for Recruiters)

  • Sales & Revenue Analytics
  • Call Center Metrics (AHT, SLA, CSAT)
  • Jewellery & Manufacturing Operations
  • Inventory & Stock Management
  • Finance & P&L Understanding
  • E-commerce Metrics (Funnel & Conversion)
⭐ Domain knowledge makes you more valuable than someone who only knows tools.
---

4. Soft Skills Recruiters Prioritize

  • Communication (Written & Verbal)
  • Stakeholder Management
  • Problem Solving
  • Attention to Detail
  • Team Collaboration
  • Time Management
  • Presentation Skills
✔ These soft skills should appear in your experience bullet points—not just in the skills list.
---

5. ATS Keywords (Must-Have for Shortlisting)

  • “Dashboard Development”
  • “Data Cleaning”
  • “Report Automation”
  • “Insights & Recommendations”
  • “KPI Tracking”
  • “Data Modelling”
  • “DAX Measures”
  • “Ad-hoc Analysis”
⭐ Use the exact wording from the JD for better ATS match.
---

6. Recommended Skill Section Format

Example (for your resume):

Tools: Excel (Advanced), SQL, Power BI, Power Query, DAX, MySQL
Analytics: Data Cleaning, Visualization, KPI Analysis, Reporting Automation
Domain: Sales, Call Center Operations, Jewellery Manufacturing
Soft Skills: Communication, Problem Solving, Time Management
✔ Keep skills grouped (Tools / Analytics / Domain / Soft Skills) ✔ Don’t write too many skills—only the ones you actually know ✔ Always match your skills with JD keywords

Adding the right skills can instantly improve your resume’s ATS score and make you stand out in MIS, Data Analyst, and BI Developer job applications.
