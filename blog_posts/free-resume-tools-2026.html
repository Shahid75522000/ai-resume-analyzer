Free Tools to Improve Your Resume (2026 List)

Here are the best free tools to rewrite, redesign, analyze and improve your resume — without spending money.

You don’t need expensive software to create a professional resume. Here are the best free resume tools for 2026 — including ATS checkers, templates, AI analyzers, grammar tools and formatting tools.

---

1. Your Own AI Resume Analyzer ❤️

This website has a built-in free AI Resume Analyzer that gives:

  • ATS Score
  • Missing skills
  • Keyword suggestions
  • Improvement plan
  • Cover letter
  • Interview questions
  • Complete improved resume

Best of all — it’s 100% free.

---

2. Google Docs — Free Resume Templates

Google Docs provides clean, ATS-friendly, free resume templates that are easy to edit.

Benefits:
  • Simple and modern designs
  • Cloud storage
  • Free export to PDF
---

3. Canva Free Templates

Canva has thousands of free resume templates. However, avoid designs with too many graphics because ATS may reject them.

Use only:
  • Simple templates
  • Minimalistic designs
  • Single-column layouts
---

4. Grammarly — Grammar & Professional Tone

Resume language must be clean, professional and mistake-free. Grammarly detects:

  • Grammar mistakes
  • Repetitive sentences
  • Missing punctuation
  • Tone issues
---

5. ChatGPT (Free Version)

ChatGPT can help with:

  • Improving summary
  • Rewriting bullet points
  • Creating achievement-focused lines
  • Matching resume to job description
---

6. Jobscan Free Version

Jobscan is a popular ATS checker that compares:

  • Your resume vs job description
  • Missing keywords
  • Skill match

The free version offers limited scans — but still very useful.

---

7. Overleaf (For CVs)

If you're applying to academic roles, Overleaf offers professional LaTeX CV templates.

Best for:
  • PhD applicants
  • Research roles
  • Teaching jobs
---

8. Microsoft Word Online (Free)

You can use Word for free through the browser. It includes dozens of modern templates.

  • Export as DOCX or PDF
  • Clean separation of sections
  • ATS-friendly layouts
---

9. FlowCV

A modern, free resume builder with minimalistic designs.

Benefits:
  • Live editing
  • One-click PDF export
  • Clean, ATS-safe templates
---

10. Resume Worded (Free Version)

  • Gives resume score
  • Checks weak bullet points
  • Suggests improvements

Paid plan has more features, but the free version is enough for most people.

---

Recommended Free Tool Stack (Best Combo)

✔ Use Your AI Resume Analyzer → For ATS score & rewrite ✔ Use Google Docs → For layout ✔ Use Grammarly → For grammar check ✔ Use ChatGPT → For summary, bullet points ✔ Use Jobscan → For keyword match

Use these tools together and your resume quality will improve massively — often enough to get shortlisted within a week.
