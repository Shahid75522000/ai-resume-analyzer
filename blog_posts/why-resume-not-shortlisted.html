Why Your Resume Is Not Getting Shortlisted (2026 Guide)

Top reasons candidates get rejected automatically by ATS + recruiters—and how to fix them fast.

If you're applying to many jobs but not getting interview calls, your resume may be getting filtered out by an ATS or rejected by recruiters within 10 seconds. Here are the most common reasons and simple fixes.

---

1. Your Resume Is Not ATS-Friendly

More than 75% of companies use ATS. Fancy templates, graphics, tables or icons break ATS scanning.

Fix:
  • Use a clean, single-column resume
  • No photos, icons or heavy graphics
  • Use simple fonts: Calibri, Arial, Verdana
---

2. Missing Job Description Keywords

ATS filters resumes based on keywords related to skills, tools and responsibilities.

Fix:
  • Add exact keywords from JD
  • Include tools like SQL, Excel, Power BI
  • Add domain terms like SLA, KPI, Reporting
---

3. Weak or Generic Summary

A poor summary makes recruiters skip your resume quickly.

Fix:
  • Write a strong 3–4 line summary
  • Add tools + experience + result
  • Example: “3 years experience in MIS, SQL, Power BI…”
---

4. No Metrics or Achievements

  • “Handled reports” → Weak
  • “Automated MIS reports reducing time by 40%” → Strong
Fix:
  • Add numbers, percentages, hours saved
  • Use action verbs (Improved, Automated, Designed)
---

5. Resume Is Too Short or Too Long

Ideal length: 1–2 pages. Less = missing details. More = overwhelming.

---

6. Applying to Wrong Job Profiles

If you’re MIS and applying to Data Science roles, you won’t get shortlisted.

Fix: Target related roles:
  • MIS → Reporting Analyst → Data Analyst → BI Analyst
---

7. Using the Same Resume for Every Job

Generic resumes don’t match specific JDs.

Fix:
  • Tailor skills section
  • Update summary for each role
  • Add relevant keywords
---

8. Poor Formatting

If your alignment, spacing or layout looks messy, recruiters reject instantly.

Fix:
  • Consistent spacing
  • Simple bullet points
  • Clear section headings
---

9. Career Gaps Not Explained

Unexplained gaps can reduce your chances.

Fix:
  • Add 1-line explanation: “Upskilling”, “Personal reasons”, “Certification”
---

10. Resume Not Mobile-Friendly

Recruiters often check resumes on mobile. If the layout breaks, they skip it.

Fix: Use PDF exported from Word/Docs—never scanned PDF.
---
✔ Use ATS-friendly template ✔ Add keywords from JD ✔ Add achievements with numbers ✔ Fix formatting & spacing ✔ Tailor resume for each job

Fixing these issues can instantly increase your shortlist chances by 50% or more.
