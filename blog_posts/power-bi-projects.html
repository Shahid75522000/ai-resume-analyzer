Top 10 Power BI Projects to Add in Your Resume

Show recruiters real, practical dashboards instead of just writing “Power BI” in your skills section.

Power BI is one of the most requested skills for Data Analyst and MIS roles. But just writing “Power BI” in your resume is not enough — you must show projects that prove you can connect data, model it and build useful dashboards.

1. Sales Performance Dashboard

What it shows: Revenue by month, region, product, top customers, YoY growth.

Skills used: Power Query, Date table, DAX (Total Sales, LY Sales, YoY%), slicers, drill-through.

2. Call Center Performance Dashboard

Metrics: AHT, Calls answered, Abandon rate, FCR, CSAT, SLA%.

Great project if you have BPO background (which you do) – connects perfectly to your experience.

3. Coffee Shop / Retail Store Analytics

Use transactional sales data with Date, Store, Product and Customer tables. Show hourly sales, weekday vs weekend, best-selling items, and combo analysis.

4. Inventory & Stock Ageing Dashboard

Especially powerful for jewellery, manufacturing and trading businesses. Show current stock, ageing buckets (0–30, 31–60, 61–90 days), slow-moving items.

5. HR Attrition & Headcount Dashboard

Metrics: Joiners, leavers, attrition %, department-wise, tenure bands.

6. Finance / P&L Dashboard

Summarise revenue, cost, profit, margin, budget vs actual, variance %.

7. Website / Marketing Performance

Use Google Analytics export or sample data: sessions, users, conversion rate, campaign, device, country.

8. Employee Productivity Dashboard

Useful for operations: tasks completed per agent, error rate, utilisation %, shift-wise comparison.

9. Customer Feedback & NPS Dashboard

Combine survey results with categories like product, location, channel, and show NPS, CSAT and themes.

10. Management MIS Dashboard (Monthly)

Combine finance + operations + quality into a single view for management. This mirrors real-world reporting and looks great on your resume.

How to write these in your resume:
  • “Built Power BI dashboard to track call center KPIs (AHT, FCR, SLA), used by managers daily.”
  • “Created sales performance report with YoY growth and top products for 20+ stores.”
  • “Designed inventory ageing report to identify slow-moving SKUs and reduce stock holding.”
