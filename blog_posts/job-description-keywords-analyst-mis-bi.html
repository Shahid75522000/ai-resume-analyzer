How to Use These Keywords in Your Resume

✔ Add 10–12 keywords from this list ✔ Match keywords directly from the Job Description ✔ Mix both technical skills (SQL, Excel) + business skills (KPI, SLA) ✔ Use them naturally in Summary, Skills & Experience sections

Including the right JD keywords will significantly increase your ATS score and improve your chances of getting shortlisted for Analyst, MIS and BI roles.
